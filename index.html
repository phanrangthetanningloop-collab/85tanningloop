How to Get from HCMC to Ninh Thuan

Getting to Ninh Thuan is easier than most people think. The province sits ~340km northeast of Ho Chi Minh City. Here are your options.

Option 1: Sleeper Bus (Recommended)

The most popular choice. Buses depart from Mien Dong bus station in HCMC every evening around 9–10pm and arrive in Phan Rang by 3–5am. Cost: 250–350k standard, 400–550k limousine. We can help you book — just ask when you make your reservation.

Option 2: Train

Thap Cham station (Ninh Thuan) is on the main Hanoi–HCMC line. From Saigon station, the journey takes 5–6 hours. Scenic — you ride along the coast for the last two hours. Book at vr.com.vn at least 3 days ahead.

Option 3: Private Car

Fastest option at around 4 hours. Best for groups of 4+ splitting the cost. Multiple services available on Grab or via your hotel.

Getting to Lodge Tan An from Phan Rang

Lodge Tan An is in Ninh Hai village, approximately 25km from Phan Rang city center. We offer pick-up from Phan Rang bus station or train station for +100,000đ/person — book this when you make your tour reservation and we'll be waiting for you.
